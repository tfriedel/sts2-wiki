## Overview

An encounter with two Chomper automatons. One of them is configured to attack first.

## Mechanics

- **Initial attack**: One Chomper has the `ScreamFirst` flag set, which means it will attack immediately at the start of combat while the other Chomper will attack on its normal turn.
- **Monster count**: Despite both monsters being the same Chomper unit, they function as separate combatants with independent health pools and turn order.
